Stefan van Vliet

Hello, I am Stefan van Vliet. I am 19 years old and I am currently studying Software Development at SiNTLUCAS in Eindhoven.

Programming is much more to me than just writing code. I enjoy turning ideas into something people can actually play or experience.

Alongside coding, I like building 3D models in Blender. I also worked on an Arduino project where software and hardware came together, which I found both challenging and exciting.

The mix of creativity and technology is exactly why I want to keep growing as a game developer.

  • Hobbies: football, gaming, and going out with friends.
  • Focus: game development with C#/Unity and 3D workflows.
  • Motivation: building projects that feel interactive and alive.
